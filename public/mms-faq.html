File Setup

What unit of measurement should my design files be set to?

What unit of measurement should my design files be set to?

We require all design files to be set in either inches (in) or millimeters (mm). Our laser cutters cannot process files that are saved in other units of measurement.

What is the minimum bridging width in a design?

What is the minimum bridging width in a design?

Any bridging elements in your design should be at least 1 mm (3/64 in) wide.

What is the maximum file size I can upload?

What is the maximum file size I can upload?

Each individual file must be no larger than 10 MB.

Can I include or nest multiple designs in one file?

Can I include or nest multiple designs in one file?

We recommend placing only non-identical parts together in one file, such as custom ornaments with different names, or a range of distinct components. If you do this, please leave at least 3mm (1/8 in) of space between each part.

If you want to order multiple copies of the same part, upload that part's design once and specify the desired quantity at checkout. We also offer volume discounts on orders of identical parts.

How do I avoid dense nodes or anchor points in my file?

How do I avoid dense nodes or anchor points in my file?

Our software automatically identifies these points and can help simplify them for smoother cutting.

Can you assist me with my design?

Can you assist me with my design?

Please refer to our design guidelines for a detailed, step-by-step process on how to prepare your files. At this time, we do not offer full design services.

Materials & Sizes

What metals are available for sheet cutting?

What metals are available for sheet cutting?

We currently offer 304 stainless steel, A36 mild steel (low-carbon),Q235 galvanized steel, 6061-T6 aluminum alloy, and 5082 Aluminum Alloy (Al-Mg). See Sheet Metal Details and Specifications.

What are the maximum part dimensions?

What are the maximum part dimensions?

For online quotes, each dimension of your project can be up to 32 inches. If your design exceeds 32 inches, a custom shipping quote will be required. We can accommodate sheet sizes up to 5 feet by 10 feet.

What are the minimum part dimensions?

What are the minimum part dimensions?

The smallest piece we can cut measures 0.25 inches by 0.375 inches. The smallest hole diameter we can cut is 1.5 millimeters (1/16 inch).

Tolerances & Standards

What are your tolerances?

What are your tolerances?

Our general tolerance for completed parts is ±0.1 mm.

Will you return any unused material or scraps?

Will you return any unused material or scraps?

We do not ship leftover scraps or dropouts once we have cut your parts.

Will my laser-cut sheet metal parts be perfectly flat?

Will my laser-cut sheet metal parts be perfectly flat?

All metals may exhibit a slight bend, but our parts are generally considered flat for most applications.

What is your material thickness tolerance?

What is your material thickness tolerance?

Our materials have a thickness tolerance of ±0.2 mm.

What is your cut tolerance?

What is your cut tolerance?

We cut parts with a tolerance of ±0.15 mm.

Are parts from MyMetalShop guaranteed to be food safe?

Are parts from MyMetalShop guaranteed to be food safe?

We cannot guarantee any level of food safety for our metal parts.

Will my parts be tapered after cutting?

Will my parts be tapered after cutting?

You may notice minor tapering based on the thickness of the material, but it is usually minimal.

Do I need to factor kerf into my design?

Do I need to factor kerf into my design?

No. Our machinery automatically compensates for kerf, and adding your own adjustments may cause parts to be larger than intended.

Edge Quality

Will my deburred parts still have sharp edges?

Will my deburred parts still have sharp edges?

All parts will go through a deburred process if you request it.

We offer a deburring process upon request, which helps smooth out the edges of your parts. Although we make every effort to minimize sharp edges, some parts may still retain areas that feel slightly sharp.

Can you chamfer or machine the edges of parts?

Can you chamfer or machine the edges of parts?

Yes. If you would like chamfered or machined edges, please include these specifications with your order.

Do you offer deburring?

Do you offer deburring?

Yes, deburring is available for an additional fee.

Do you provide tumbling?

Do you provide tumbling?

Will my parts have burr and dross?

Will my parts have burr and dross?

Laser cutting is highly accurate and produces very little dross. If you request deburring, we will make every effort to ensure edges are smooth; however, some parts may still have minor sharp spots.

Do you offer beveling or 3D cutting?
