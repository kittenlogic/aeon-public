About Fiber Lasers

What material types can you work with?

  • Metals: Aluminum, Gold, Silver, Copper, Stainless Steel, Brass, Titanium and Tungsten
  • Nonmetals: Colored Acrylic, Hard Plastics, Brick, Granite, Marble, Tile, Nylon, ABS, Ceramic, River Rock, Precious Stones, Marble, Artificial Leather and Cardboard

What are the advantages of a fiber laser?

  • Mark on metals and nonmetals
  • Easy to install and portable
  • Extremely fast and precise
  • 100,000 hour lifespan
  • Zero consumables and easy to maintain
  • Compatible with Lightburn Software
