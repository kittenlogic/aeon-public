About Us

Our Company

Aeon Laser USA is affiliated with Engraving Machines Plus, Corp, established in 2008 in Melbourne, Florida. EMP is committed to creating jobs in the USA that pay a living wage, promote a healthy and happy work environment, and create a supportive culture for our customers by offering ongoing education, resources, and opportunties to thrive.

Our Brand

With over 13 years of experience, we know CO2 lasers inside and out. From troubleshooting to innovation, we strive to bring you superior products and intelligent solutions that make working with your laser a joy. We believe that Aeon lasers are among the best machines we have worked with and can't wait to show you why!

Our Commitment to You

New to CO2 lasers? Or looking to upgrade your existing CO2 laser cutter? Whatever your goals, we've helped thousands of people find the right machine to fit their needs. We listen to our customers and provide them with the means to succeed. Learn more about the exclusive benefits we offer our customers.

Our Story

  • SEPT 2008 Engraving Machines Plus, Corp (EMP) is founded by Danny and Steven in a duplex with 2 laptops and $400 startup money. Products: rotary engravers. Employees: 2
  • DEC 2010 EMP becomes the top Roland dealer for engraving machines in the USA by offering free training and support.
  • MAY 2013 $1M in revenue, EMP upgrades to beach front condo. Employees: 3
  • JUNE 2015 Danny refinances his vehicles and makes first inventory investment in a 100 sq/ft storage unit.
  • JULY 2016 EMP expands product line to include CO2 lasers. A spare bedroom in Danny's condo becomes the laser room.
  • DEC 2016 EMP signs lease for 3,000 sq/ft warehouse and orders first 20ft GP container of Bodor lasers from China.
  • SUMMER 2017 EMP launches R&D department in Danny’s kitchen with European YouTube sensation Russ Sadler.
  • OCT 2018 EMP partners with March of Dimes in an ongoing effort to improve the health of mothers and babies.
  • FEB 2019 Aeon Laser USA opens 1200 sq/ft showroom in Melbourne, FL.
  • APRIL 2019 EMP solidifies exclusivity with Aeon China at the ISA Show in Las Vegas. Aeon Laser USA is born!
  • DEC 2019 EMP develops revolutionary technology that will change the future of lasers as we know it.
  • JAN 2020 EMP receives the first 40ft HQ container of Aeon Lasers before the start of the pandemic.
  • FEB 2020 Aeon Laser USA develops handmade wiring harnesses to increase safety and ease of end user repair.
  • MARCH 2020 Aeon Laser USA begins the production of PPE to raise money for first responders.
  • JUNE 2020 EMP begins construction on a 22,500 sq/ft. facility with the aim to bring affordable laser manufacturing to the US.
  • JULY 2020 That Mom With a Laser visits Aeon Laser USA and we go viral!
  • AUG 2021 Aeon Laser USA launches partnership program.
  • DEC 2021 Aeon Laser USA celebrates $10M in revenue by hosting a Holiday Weekend for it’s Partners and Staff. Employees: 35+
  • JAN 2022 EMP partners with HSG to distribute industrial grade lasers in the US.
  • FEB 2022 EMP receives the first of many pieces of metal fabrication equipment to begin US manufacturing in 2024.
  • TODAY We are moving into our new facility where we will start to build our own components and bring much of our manufacturing and assembly here to the US!
