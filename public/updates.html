Exciting Updates to After Sales Support Options Starting January 1, 2025

Latest update: December 13, 2024

We’re thrilled to announce an enhancement to our after sales support experience!

Beginning January 1, 2025, Aeon Laser USA will introduce two paid customer support services, which in effect will streamline the existing complimentary customer support experience. These services are designed to provide you with even more flexibility and personalized care, ensuring you can choose the best possible support tailored to your needs.

Helping Hand Sessions

Customers will have the option to purchase dedicated time slots with a qualified technician for non-warranty-related needs. These include; but are not limited to, assistance with:

Lightburn setup and navigation.
General use questions.
Dialing in settings.
Implementation of add-ons (i.e. rotary attachments, exhaust setups, and air compressors).
Additional guidance with documented processes in our knowledgebase (i.e. laser tube replacement, routine maintenance, and repairs).

Priority Passes ($75/hr.)

Additionally, we will be rolling out a service that allows customers to purchase expedited guidance for time critical warranty-related troubleshooting and repairs.This new service ensures customers have the option to:

Completely bypass triage and initial troubleshooting via email communication.
Schedule a personalized care session with an appointed senior level technician.
Quickly achieve a diagnosis with minimal back-and-forth communication

Streamlined Support

With these two new services, we aim to streamline our support process and further enhance your experience. By addressing time-critical and non-warranty-related needs separately, our frontline team can stay focused on managing incoming emails and support tickets more effectively. This approach will lead to faster response times and quicker resolutions for all support cases. Going forward, here’s what you can expect when contacting support:

Triage and Initial Communication

Complimentary technical support will begin by submitting a support ticket request. Our frontline team will initiate email correspondence to assess the issue and guide you to relevant troubleshooting or repair resources in our knowledgebase. This approach ensures an efficient first step toward resolving your concerns.

Personalized Care Sessions

If the issue cannot be clearly diagnosed via email, meaning there is a lack of documentation surrounding the issue, or the provided documentation does not yield the intended resolution, customers under warranty will be invited to schedule a personalized care session with a senior technician. These sessions are provided at no additional charge and are designed to deliver tailored, one-on-one support.

Escalations

If a Personalized Care Session does not fully diagnose or resolve the issue, your case will be escalated to Upper Management. At which point, additional actions, such as dispatching a technician to perform an on-site diagnosis or repair, will be considered and discussed with you.

If a technician is dispatched, your continued cooperation will be essential to help narrow down potential causes and ensure that our emergency response team arrives prepared with a plan of action that has the best chance for success.

Support for Out-of-Warranty Machines

Even after your warranty expires, you’ll retain unlimited access to our elite frontline via email for assistance with any machine related issues. If a personalized care session is desired or required, it can be scheduled by purchasing a Priority Pass, which offers premium, one-on-one support.

Should the issue remain unresolved during the paid session, you have the option to extend the session for additional time, which will be billed accordingly. If the session still does not result in a resolution, we can discuss and provide a quote for scheduling an on-site visit to address the problem directly.

Why This Change?

We remain dedicated to delivering the exceptional after-sales support that has established us as one of the “World’s Greatest” companies in the industry. However, as our Aeon family continues to grow, we’re met with the challenge of finding ways to scale our operation without compromising the quality of service our customers have come to know and love. By enhancing and streamlining our support processes, we aim to provide even greater value:

Efficiency - Our frontline will focus on handling less critical support tickets more effectively, improving response times and minimizing overall downtime.

Flexibility - Customers needing extra care or immediate guidance will be segmented and prioritized, ensuring their issues are addressed promptly.

Expansion - Our resource team will continue to expand and refine our comprehensive knowledgebase, promoting efficiency and benefiting all customers with quicker, self-guided solutions.

These exciting new changes reflect our dedication to delivering unparalleled customer service. It empowers our Aeon Family to customize their support experience while helping us grow and maintain the high standard of care Aeon Laser USA is known for.

Stay tuned for more details about these services, pricing, and our updated support and warranty policies. We’ll be sharing all the information you need as the launch date approaches!

Thank you for being part of our journey to deliver the best in laser technology and support.
