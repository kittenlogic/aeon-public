AEON is Innovation

User-centered R&D

Aeon Laser USA is involved in an ongoing effort to bridge the communication gap between our factory and our end users. Our domestic R&D team exhibits a deeply rooted passion to not only develop new products, but to also make them adaptable to existing ones. In fact, most of the ideas for our unique innovations come from within our community. We’re always looking for ways to enhance your experience, long after you’ve made your purchase.

Our Technology

Wiring

US designed and engineered wiring harness for the MIRA Pro series, combines multiple wires into a single less-flexible bundle. The result is fewer quality issues and an added layer of safety in comparison to loose wires.

Smart Air

Air plays a major role in laser engraving and cutting and varies from one material to another. That’s why we install our own solenoid valves in every laser. It’s an intelligent way to toggle between low and high pressure air seamlessly via Lightburn. Without this, you’ll have to manually adjust your air between layers, which is less efficient.

Enhanced Cutting

When MIRA owners expressed the need to cut through thicker materials, we got right to work and developed a 4” lens kit to fit all existing models. Some customers have even reported cutting up to 1” thick birchwood!

Zone Control

Your laser’s performance is only as good as its ability to remove smoke efficiently. Lingering smoke is not only a health hazard, it also contributes to inconsistent results, more frequent maintenance and excessive scrubbing of your finished products. Our in-house, US Made Iris, maximizes fume extraction to help keep you, your laser, and your finished products in a smoke free environment. Available for NOVA lasers.

Multi-Roller

With our latest US Made Multi-Roller, you can now reclaim the time wasted hovering over your laser, and start batching out your tumbler orders in bulk. Quickly load up to six 20oz or 30oz tumblers all at the same time. Available for MIRA and NOVA lasers.
