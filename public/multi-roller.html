Your Tumbler Game is About to Change!

The days of hovering over your laser, engraving tumblers one-by-one, are finally over!

If you’ve ever had to engrave a case of tumblers, you know just how time consuming and tiresome it can be.

It’s not because they take long – afterall, Aeon offers the fastest tumbler cycle times of any laser in its class. It’s simply due to having to load them in the machine one at a time and not having enough time between engraves to do much else.

Meet the Multi-Roller, an industry first that allows you to quickly load up to six 20oz or 30oz tumblers all at the same time!

Say goodbye to hovering and hello to increased productivity!

Pre-Order Today!

So how easy is it to use the Multi-Roller?

It is by far the easiest rotary to setup in the market. NO clamps, NO adjustment knobs, NO leveling, NO blade removal, just plug and play.

Made for Tumblers

The Multi-Roller is specifically designed for 20oz and 30oz tumblers. Switching between the two is a breeze and ensures each cup is in the perfect location at the perfect angle. Best of all, it was designed to fit in all of our Aeon laser models and without having to do much prep work other than lowering your table and plugging it in.

Pre-made Lightburn Templates

Wait, it gets even better. The Multi-Roller comes with premade Lightburn templates so there’s no measuring or framing involved. Simply load the file, import your images, and center them in their predesignated areas. Each design can be the same or completely different.

Check out our FAQ

What laser models with the Multi-Roller work with?

The Multi-Roller will work only in US Aeon Mira and Nova laser models.

How long does it take to engrave 6 cups?

It depends on the extent of the artwork, the sample below of six 20oz tumblers with a 1.15" x 4" Aeon Laser USA logo engraved on it, took 11 minutes on the MIRA.

Can it be adapted to work in another laser brand?

No, the Muli-Roller was designed to only work with Aeon Lasers.

What prevents the cups from slipping?

The Multi-Roller uses silicone rollers and ultra low drag bearings to ensure more contact area across the entire cup instead of just the rim.

Can it accommodate other items other than tumblers?

No, the Multi-Roller was designed primarily for 20oz. and 30oz. tumblers.

Can it engrave a mixture of 20oz. and 30oz. tumblers?

No, the Multi-Roller must be configured for one size or the other. It’s easy to switch between the two sizes, you just can’t do both at the same time.

What brand tumblers is it compatible with?

We ran successful tests with YETI, RTIC and Polar. It’s very likely other brands will work as well, but we recommend sending in a sample for testing if you want to be sure.

Can I engrave different designs on each cup or do they all need to be the same?

Yes, all 6 cups can have unique designs or they can all be identical, the choice is yours.
