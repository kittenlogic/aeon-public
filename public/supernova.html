What is the difference between the NOVA and Super NOVA?

The standard NOVA comes fitted with a glass DC laser tube, stepper motors, and can achieve max speeds of up to 1200mm/sec.

The Super NOVA comes with two laser sources (metal RF and glass DC), hybrid stepper motors, and can achieve max speeds of up to 2000mm/sec.

What is the purpose of combining two laser sources into one machine?

Metal RF laser tubes can produce greater detail and are able to be pulsed much faster than glass DC laser tubes. Therefore, they are better suited for engraving at high speeds and last 3-5x longer that a glass DC laser tube.

On the other hand, glass DC laser tubes are superior for cutting applications and are far more affordable to replace, especially when you get into the higher wattages.

By combining the two into one machine, you get the best of both worlds!

Will I have the option to use the glass DC laser tube for engraving or the metal RF laser tube for cutting?

Yes, you can choose whether to use the metal RF or glass DC tube in the software. Either laser tube can be set to engrave or cut.

What sizes is the Aeon Super NOVA offered in?

The Aeon Super NOVA is available in 3 different sizes:

NOVA10S = 700mm x 1000mm (approx. 28" x 40")
NOVA14S = 900mm x 1400mm (approx. 36" x 55")
NOVA16S = 1000mm x 1600mm (approx. 40" x 63")

What wattage options are available for the Aeon Super NOVA?

The RF metal laser tube for engraving, is offered in 30W and 60W configurations of the Aeon Super NOVA. The DC glass laser tube for cutting is offered in the range of 90W-180W.

Does the Aeon Super NOVA run off 120v or 240v?

Either one is available depending on what country you are in. In the United States, all Aeon Super NOVAs will come wired for 120 volts, which is the voltage produced by a standard outlet.

What is Active Airflow?

Active Airflow is another Aeon innovation used to improve the performance of the exhaust system by force feeding makeup air into the cabinet via a series of tiny fans. The faster air enters and exits the cabinet, the cleaner your results and the less cleaning there is to do.

What is Seamless Source Switching (SSS)?

Seamless Source Switching is a proprietary technology Aeon developed to switch between the RF metal tube and the DC glass tube. It is all programmed via the software and happens in an instant.

What is the Super Clean Pack design all about?

The Super Clean Pack Design takes the standard clean pack design from previous generations one step further. Not only are the rails and bearings protected along the X and Y axis, but a new curtain system is used to seal off the rest of the cabinet from the interior work space.

Does the All-in-One System make it harder to maintain and repair those components?

No, the 5200 chiller, exhaust fan and air pump are easily accessible and can be removed in just a few minutes.

How is the integrated auto focus better than previous versions?

The new integrated auto focus uses the actual nozzle from the laser head to sense the material surface versus a separate probe. The new design is less prone to snagging on materials and failing due to a build up of debris.

What is the difference between the Ruida controller on the Aeon Super NOVA versus the previous NOVA?

The new Ruida controller has direct access to more popular functions that were previously buried in the Z/U menu. It will also display both the mA reading of your laser tube and the water temperature reading of the onboard 5200 chiller.

How does the Lightburn Camera work?

The Lightburn Camera captures a still Birdseye image of any item you place inside your laser and then allows you to visually overlay your design on top of it. This helps you to quickly and accurately position artwork, which eliminates tedious guess work and reduces failed engraving attempts.

Should I still get an air compressor to assist with cutting applications?

Yes, increasing the air flow by means of compressed air can assist with cutting applications. The standard air pump is only designed to prevent the focal lens from getting dirty and ensure the laser beam has a clear path to the material, without reflecting off of any smoke. For the Aeon Super NOVA, we recommend the 26 gallon Kobalt Quiet Tech from Lowes.

Does the Aeon Super NOVA have a pass through?

Yes, a full front to rear pass through is standard on the Aeon Super NOVA.

Does the Aeon Super NOVA fit through a standard doorway?

Yes, a standard doorway typically measures anywhere between 30" - 36". The bottom portion of the Aeon Super NOVA can be easily removed, leaving the top portion at just under 30" tall, once turned on its side.
