Features

Meet the Super NOVA, Aeon’s newest addition to our laser family! It’s full of all sorts of new goodies that will make any laser owner fall in love.

The Super NOVA combines the best of both worlds with its innovative dual source design.

Finally, a laser that combines the ultra high resolution and speeds customary of metal RF laser tubes, with the superb cutting performance that only a glass DC laser tube can provide.

Matched with hybrid stepper motors and a newly designed enclosed rail system, the Super NOVA is ready to take your laser engraving and cutting business to the next level.

Benefits

Increased ROI

It’s quite simple, the less time you spend in front of your laser, the greater your return on investment. With the addition of a fast pulsing, RF metal laser tube, we’ve upgraded to hybrid stepper motors to match. Prepare for takeoff, with maximum scan speeds of up to 2000 mm/sec! It's time to enjoy shorter cycle times, which equals more money in your pocket.

Less Downtime

With increased productivity, an increase in maintenance and repairs is likely to follow. Having all of the major drive components protected from soot and debris, helps keep downtime to a minimum. Aeon’s proactive approach helps you spend less time cleaning and maintaining your equipment, and more time cranking out orders.

Super Clean Pack Design

Aeon’s signature clean pack technology has taken the next step in the evolutionary process. Not only are the linear rails and bearing blocks enclosed (as in previous models), but protective curtains on the left and right side rails now prevent unwanted particles from spreading beyond your work area. Enjoy greater peace of mind with a laser system you can truly rely on.

Fastest in its Class

Aeon’s newly designed, lightweight laser head, paired with high speed, hybrid stepper motors, is sure to leave the competition in the dust. Experience an astonishing blur of acceleration with top speeds reaching up to 2000 mm/sec.

Seamless Source Switching (SSS)

Switching between the RF metal tube and the DC glass tube, happens seamlessly via a motorized first mirror. The software automatically triggers the appropriate laser tube and mirror position in roughly half a second.

All-in-One System

Installing a large format laser in a confined space, has never been more practical. Enjoy a clutter free workspace with a built-in 5200 water chiller, exhaust fan and air pump. All components are still easily accessible and can be removed for routine maintenance.

Integrated Auto Focus

The newly designed laser head features an integrated auto focusing mechanism that is lightweight and a whole lot more reliable. It’s encapsulated right inside the nozzle, to shield it from smoke and debris. Say goodbye to collisions and gouged material.

Advanced Controls

Another industry first is the all new redesigned Ruida controller, which brings commonly used functions right to the surface, for increased setup times and overall ease of use. You can even monitor amperage (mA) and water temperature right from the display screen.

Software

Achieve maximum potential with Lightburn, now included with every Aeon product. Use the camera overlay to quickly position artwork over oddly shaped items, or cut out preprinted materials with accuracy, using the print to cut feature.

Multiple Interfaces

Easily send jobs from your PC or Mac via USB, Ethernet or WiFi. There is even 256 MB of onboard memory built right into the controller for storing popular jobs, or you can even run programs straight from a USB Flash Drive.

Scrap Drawer

Cleanup is a breeze with a newly integrated scrap drawer. All of your cut pieces now fall into a conveniently located compartment below, which can be easily emptied to prevent scrap pieces from piling up and becoming a fire hazard.

Active Airflow

Say goodbye to excessive soot buildup on your material and in your laser cabinet. A series of tiny fans are now used to force feed makeup air into the laser cabinet, which inturn improves how quickly smoke is evacuated from the system. The more air you move across the work table, the cleaner the results, which means less time scrubbing for you.

Product Specs

What is the difference between the NOVA and Super NOVA?

The standard NOVA will come fitted with a glass DC laser tube, stepper motors, and will achieve max speeds of up to 1200mm/sec.

The Super NOVA will come with two laser sources (metal RF and glass DC), hybrid stepper motors, and will achieve max speeds of up to 2000mm/sec.

What is the purpose of combining two laser sources into one machine?

Metal RF laser tubes can produce greater detail and are able to be pulsed much faster than glass DC laser tubes. Therefore, they are better suited for engraving at high speeds and last 3-5x longer that a glass DC laser tube.

On the other hand, glass DC laser tubes are superior for cutting applications and are far more affordable to replace, especially when you get into the higher wattages.

By combining the two into one machine, you get the best of both worlds!

Will I have the option to use the glass DC laser tube for engraving or the metal RF laser tube for cutting?

Yes, you can choose whether to use the metal RF or glass DC tube in the software. Either laser tube can be set to engrave or cut.

What sizes will the Super NOVA be offered in?

The Super NOVA will be available in 3 different sizes:

NOVA10 = 700mm x 1000mm (approx. 28" x 40")
NOVA14 = 900mm x 1400mm (approx. 36" x 55")
NOVA16 = 1000mm x 1600mm (approx. 40" x 63")

What wattage options will the Super NOVA be available in?

The RF metal laser tube for engraving, will be offered in 30W and 60W configurations. The DC glass laser tube for cutting will be offered in the range of 90W-180W.

Will the new Super NOVA run off 120v or 240v?

Either one is available depending on what country you are in. In the United States, all Super NOVAs will come wired for 120 volts, which is the voltage produced by a standard outlet.

What is Active Airflow?

Active Airflow is another Aeon innovation used to improve the performance of the exhaust system by force feeding makeup air into the cabinet via a series of tiny fans. The faster air enters and exits the cabinet, the cleaner your results and the less cleaning there is to do.

What is Seamless Source Switching (SSS)?

Seamless source switching is a proprietary technology Aeon developed to switch between the RF metal tube and the DC glass tube. It is all programmed via the software and happens in an instant.

What is the Super Clean Pack design all about?

This takes the standard clean pack design from previous generations one step further. Not only are the rails and bearings protected along the X and Y axis, but a new curtain system is used to seal off the rest of the cabinet from the interior work space.

Does the All-in-One System make it harder to maintain and repair those components?

No, the 5200 chiller, exhaust fan and air pump are easily accessible and can be removed in just a few minutes.

How is the integrated auto focus better than previous versions?

The new integrated auto focus uses the actual nozzle from the laser head to sense the material surface versus a separate probe. The new design is less prone to snagging on materials and failing due to a build up of debris.

What is the difference between the Ruida controller on the Super NOVA versus the previous NOVA?

The new Ruida controller will have direct access to more popular functions that were previously buried in the Z/U menu. It will also display both the mA reading of your laser tube and the water temperature reading of the onboard 5200 chiller.

How does the Lightburn Camera work?

The Lightburn Camera captures a still Birdseye image of any item you place inside your laser and then allows you to visually overlay your design on top of it. This helps you to quickly and accurately position artwork, which eliminates tedious guess work and reduces failed engraving attempts.

Should I still get an air compressor to assist with cutting applications?

Yes, increasing the air flow by means of compressed air, can assist with cutting applications. The standard air pump is only designed to prevent the focal lens from getting dirty and ensure the laser beam has a clear path to the material, without reflecting off of any smoke. For the Super NOVA, we recommend the 26 gallon Kobalt Quiet Tech from Lowes.

Will the Super NOVA have a pass through?

Yes, a full front to rear pass through is standard.

Will the Super NOVA fit through a standard doorway?

Yes, a standard doorway typically measures anywhere between 30" - 36". The bottom portion of the Super NOVA can be easily removed, leaving the top portion at just under 30" tall, once turned on its side.

Is a new NOVA being released?

Yes, in addition to the Super NOVA, Aeon has also decided to revamp the standard NOVA professional series. Information and pricing will be available shortly after the Super NOVA is released.
