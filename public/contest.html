Photo + Video Tips

How to enter

There are 50+ ways to enter. Many of the entries will ask you to create and share new videos, photos, and blog posts. We've put together some tips to help you make great content! Please refer to these tips as needed and note any additional requirements per entry. Following these guidelines is very important. Incomplete entries will be disqualified.

📸 Photo Tips

  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • NO FILTERS
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • CENTER THE PHOTO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED INTO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • PHOTOS NEED TO BE AT LEAST 1080x1080px

📹 Video Tips

  • CLEAN GLASS LID (ON BOTH SIDES)
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • STABILIZE THE PHONE BEFORE SHOOTING
  • TRY NOT TO USE THE "SELFIE CAMERA"
  • CENTER THE VIDEO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED TO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • DO NOT SPEED UP/SLOW DOWN THE VIDEOS
  • TRY TO SPEAK CLOSER TO THE CAMERA AND/OR PROJECT YOUR VOICE FOR CLEAR AUDIO
  • SET UP YOUR SHOTS SO THAT THE LASER IS IN THE FRAME WITH YOU AND YOU ARE IN THE FRAME WITH THE LASER :)

Instructions for Photo Entries on Facebook or Instragram

  • Your Facebook post or Instagram profile must be set to public. Photo entries will not work if your profile or post is private.
  • Photo entries for Facebook and Instagram will say "hashtag can't be found" by default.
  • Once a photo is uploaded with the hashtag #AEONVIP to Facebook or Instagram, click on the entry title to select your photo.
  • If you are already viewing the Requirements for a photo entry, press "cancel" first, then click on the title to re-scan for eligible photos.
  • If you uploaded more than one photo with the hashtag #AEONVIP, be sure to select the correct photo for the entry.
