How To Enter

How to Get More Entries

The more ways you enter, the greater your chances are of winning! Please follow these guidelines to maximize your chances:

  1. Read and follow the requirements per entry carefully. Incomplete entries will be disqualified.
  2. You are allowed to submit one entry per category every 24 hours (max 7 entries per category). To take advantage of these daily entries, please select a new project. NO DUPLICATES. Duplicate entries will be disqualified.
  3. BONUS: complete all possible entries for the day and DOUBLE your chances! 🤩
  4. If you have issues with the contest app, please see troubleshooting tips below.

📸 Photo Tips

  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • NO FILTERS
  • NO WATERMARKS
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • CENTER THE PHOTO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED INTO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • PHOTOS NEED TO BE AT LEAST 1080x1080px

📹 Video Tips

  • CLEAN GLASS LID (ON BOTH SIDES)
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • STABILIZE THE PHONE BEFORE SHOOTING
  • TRY NOT TO USE THE "SELFIE CAMERA"
  • CENTER THE VIDEO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED TO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • DO NOT SPEED UP/SLOW DOWN THE VIDEOS
  • TRY TO SPEAK CLOSER TO THE CAMERA AND/OR PROJECT YOUR VOICE FOR CLEAR AUDIO
  • SET UP YOUR SHOTS SO THAT THE LASER IS IN THE FRAME WITH YOU AND YOU ARE IN THE FRAME WITH THE LASER :)

How to Enter from a Facebook or Instragram Account

  1. Your Facebook post or Instagram profile must be set to public. Entries will not work if your profile or post is private.
  2. Photo entries for Facebook and Instagram will say "hashtag can't be found" by default.
  3. Once your content is posted with the specified hashtag to Facebook or Instagram, click on the entry prompt in the contest app to select your entry.
  4. If you are already viewing the Requirements for an entry prompt, press "cancel" first, then click on the entry prompt to re-scan for eligible posts.
  5. If you have more than one post with the specified hashtag, be sure to select the correct post before submitting your entry.

How to Format URLs for Youtube Entries

  1. For regular Youtube video entries, you must use the URL format: "https://youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXXXXXXX" ONLY. Do NOT use this format: "https://youtu.be/XXXXXXXXXXX" or any other variation, the contest app will not recognize them.
  2. For Youtube Shorts entries, use the URL format, "https://www.youtube.com/shorts/XXXXXXXXXX"

How to Switch linked Social Accounts

To switch social profiles, follow these steps:

  1. Log out of your social profiles (Facebook, Instagram) and the contest app (Gleam).
  2. Log back into the contest app using your email address only (you may be emailed a code for verification).
  3. Once you are logged in, click "Edit" and unlink any social profiles you want to remove.
  4. Try to submit a FB or IG entry and you will be prompted to reconnect your FB or IG account.

How The Winner is Selected

  • Aeon Laser USA will be selecting the winner(s) at random. Aeon Laser USA will notify the winner(s) by email and DM. Content shared using #aeonvip will be used by Aeon Laser USA.

Good luck!
