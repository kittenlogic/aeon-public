How To Enter

How to Get More Entries

The more ways you enter, the greater your chances are of winning! Please follow these guidelines to maximize your chances:

  1. Follow the photo and video tips below. 👇
  2. Follow instructions for Facebook and Instagram posts, Youtube videos, and YouTube shorts below. 👇
  3. Read and follow the requirements per entry carefully. Incomplete entries will be disqualified.
  4. Entries reset every 24 hours, which means you can upload new content every day for every prompt.
  5. You CAN enter the same piece of content to multiple channels in the same day, but you CAN NOT submit duplicate content accross multiple days. Duplicate entries will be disqualified.
  6. BONUS: complete all possible entries for the day and DOUBLE your chances! 🤩

📸 Photo Tips

  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • NO FILTERS
  • NO WATERMARKS
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • CENTER THE PHOTO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED INTO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • PHOTOS NEED TO BE AT LEAST 1080x1080px

📹 Video Tips

  • CLEAN GLASS LID (ON BOTH SIDES)
  • WATCH FOR GLARE/REFLECTION ON THE LID
  • NATURAL LIGHTING IS THE BEST LIGHTING
  • STABILIZE THE PHONE BEFORE SHOOTING
  • TRY NOT TO USE THE "SELFIE CAMERA"
  • CENTER THE VIDEO SO IT CAN BE CROPPED TO A SQUARE IF NEEDED
  • DO NOT SPEED UP/SLOW DOWN THE VIDEOS
  • TRY TO SPEAK CLOSER TO THE CAMERA AND/OR PROJECT YOUR VOICE FOR CLEAR AUDIO
  • SET UP YOUR SHOTS SO THAT THE LASER IS IN THE FRAME WITH YOU AND YOU ARE IN THE FRAME WITH THE LASER :)

Photo Entries on Facebook and Instragram

  1. Your Facebook post or Instagram profile must be set to public. Photo entries will not work if your profile or post is private.
  2. Photo entries for Facebook and Instagram will say "hashtag can't be found" by default.
  3. Once a photo is uploaded with the specified hashtag to Facebook or Instagram, click on the entry title to select your photo.
  4. If you are already viewing the Requirements for a photo entry, press "cancel" first, then click on the title to re-scan for eligible photos.
  5. If you uploaded more than one photo with the specified hashtag, be sure to select the correct photo for the entry.

How to switch linked social accounts

To switch social profiles, follow these steps:

  1. Log out of your social profiles (Facebook, Instagram) and the contest app (Gleam).
  2. Log back into the contest app using your email address only (you will be emailed a code for verification).
  3. Once you are logged in, click "Edit" and unlink any social profiles you want to remove.
  4. Try to submit a FB or IG photo entry and you will be prompted to reconnect your FB or IG account.

Entries on Youtube

  1. For regular Youtube video entries, you must use the URL format: "https://youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXXXXXXX" ONLY. Do NOT use this format: "https://youtu.be/XXXXXXXXXXX" or any other variation, the contest app will not recognize them.
  2. For Youtube Shorts, use the URL format, "https://www.youtube.com/shorts/XXXXXXXXXX"

Winner
